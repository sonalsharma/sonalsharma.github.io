Sunita's Sewing and Alterations - Website Design

After creating the visual identity of Sunita's I had the chance to design a website for the business. I chose to create a simple and clean one page layout showing all useful information on the small studio, putting emphasis on its local roots and elegant design. The design includes a large landing page, a lightbox image gallery, illustrative icons and an interactive map. The end result was well liked by the client. I've also been given the opportunity to code the site, so I'm looking forward to implementing my design choices and be able to further my coding skills, which is what I love to do!
