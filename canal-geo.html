Canal Geomatics - Site Redesign

Canal Geomatics is a Gatineau based GPS distributor, and their website holds information on the products they have in stock. Customers may choose the product of their liking and then call the main office to order the product. They recently tasked me to redesign their website so it would have a more current look and feel. They wished to have a stronger coherence between their brand and the website, so the current design includes a lot of red and black. I'm also going for a more simple and put together look, so that their customers will have no problems navigating the site. The site is completely responsive.
