Sunita's Sewing and Alterations

Sunita's is an Ottawa based sewing and alterations studio. The aim was the rebrand their visual identity to something that is modern and yet timeless. The client had lots of ideas about where they wanted to go visually and after many iterations, the current brand was born. The logo is a mix of fun and business, much like the clien't personality.
