About Sonal

I'm a creator, a dreamer and someone who's always thinking about the future and planning every little detail.

I've been interested in playing with code and creating websites since I was a young forum crawler. Naturally my post secondary education would have to let me harness that creativity and channel it into clean and aesthetically pleasing design solutions. I design for simplicity - I love readable type and non-clashing colors. But that doesn't mean i'm afraid to take risks.

I live in between the realms of going with the flow and planning everything. I start with a plan and see where it takes me. I think a lot of my designs reflect that.

I love:

letterpress design, spending hours (or days!) coding a website til its beautiful, motion graphics, fluffy cats and gaming into 5am coma

What i'm doing now:

Sprawled out on comfy blankets, playing hacked split screen games on a projector with a killer sound system, and forgetting my tea on the side table
