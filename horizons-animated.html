Horizons Animated Process

Policy Horizons Canada (Horizons) is a small scanning and foresight group located in Ottawa, Canada. The project was to create icons for their work process and create a small video animation. Each icon illustrates a different planning stage, followed by the official Horizons logo. The icons were also to be used on the Horizons website and presentations for communication purposes.
