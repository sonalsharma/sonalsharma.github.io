Behind the screen

Hi! I’m Sonal, a designer and a front end web developer from Ottawa, Canada.

I started out as a little forum crawler in the early 2000’s, learning basic Photoshop skills to make pretty graphics for my gaming signatures. I abused those free DeviantArt brushes, a lot. I realized that I could further my skills when other gamers started asking me to make their tags. Soon I learned to take my passion for creativity and channel it into creating clean and aesthetically pleasing designs that were functional. Not only did I increase my level of expertise in Photoshop, I also learned to use the entire Creative Suite, create beautiful websites with HTML & CSS, and even dabbled in 2d animation (which fascinates me to no end).

When i’m not fussing over my design work, you can usually find me relaxing with tea and comfy blankets, playing hacked split screen games on a projector with a killer sound system.
