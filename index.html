Hello, I'm Sonal

I'm a creator, a dreamer and someone who lives in between the realms of going with the flow and planning everything. I start with a plan and see where it takes me, and I think a lot of my designs reflect that. I've been interested in playing with code and creating websites since I was a young forum crawler. My post secondary education let me harness that creativity and channel it into clean and aesthetically pleasing design solutions. I design for simplicity - I love readable type and non-clashing colors. But that doesn't mean i'm afraid to take risks. I love letterpress design, spending days coding a site to make it beautiful, motion graphics and fluffy viking cats. These days you can find me curled up in a blanket nest, playing hacked split screen games on a projector with a killer sound system and forgetting my tea on the side table.
