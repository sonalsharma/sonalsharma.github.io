DataCats Website

DataCats is an Open Data solutions provider. They needed a redesign of their site to reflect their growing company, so I built a fully responsive corporate style website. The design needed to look professional and modern, and be easy to use for current and future clients. My design combines subtle animations and a minimalist concept to create a look that is appropriate for a company working with cities and governments.

Open Guelph Website

The City of Guelph required an Open Data portal, and I was tasked with the design. The goal was to maintain their general branding and colors, while creating a site where large sets of information and data can be accessed easily. Future Of Asia Interactive Report

Policy Horizons Canada teamed up with the Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet in Australia to produce this joint foresight study exploring the forces of change shaping Asia and implications for Canada and Australia over the next 10 to 15 years. This supplementary report provides more detailed information on the issues raised in The Future of Asia: Forces of Change and Potential Surprises foresight report. The report is a 62 page pdf to be read online on Horizons' website. This project was created as part of the communications team at Horizons. I was tasked with creating the cover and the typographical layout of the report. The goal of the project was to create a design and layout that refers to the original report but has its own unique elements.

Sunita's Sewing Branding

Sunita's Sewing and Alterations is a home based studio for custom clothing and alterations. After creating a new studio space for their work, a new visual identity was also needed to help establish their brand. I was tasked with creating a new logo that was both current and timeless. I had a lot of fun incorporating the client's personality into this brand. The end result was a handmade script for the main name and Futura, a timeless sans serif, for the tagline.
