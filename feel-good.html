Feel Good Inc - Handmade Type

This is something that happened on a whim for me. I love handmade type, though admittedly don't practice it very often. I was taking a creative break one day and listening to the Gorillaz at the same time, and while listening to the song titled the same, I created this. I immediately fell in love with the initial type and decided to do something more with it. The end result was something like a logotype, and I thought it would be best to turn it into a product label for an alcoholic beverage.

I love the hard and rustic feel this logo has, it makes me think of old tattoo shops. After creating this piece of work I definitely want to keep creating more handmade type!
