Future of Asia: Supplementary Report

Policy Horizons Canada teamed up with the Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet in Australia to produce this joint foresight study exploring the forces of change shaping Asia and implications for Canada and Australia over the next 10 to 15 years. This supplementary report provides more detailed information on the issues raised in The Future of Asia: Forces of Change and Potential Surprises foresight report. The report is a 62 page pdf to be read online on Horizons' website.

The goal of the project was to create a design and layout that refers to the original report but has its own unique elements. The final report is a fully interactive pdf file that can be found at: The Future of Asia: Forces of Change and Potential Surprises - Supplementary report
