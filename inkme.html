InkMe App Design

InkMe is an iOS app that lets you find the best tattoo artist in your area based on the tattoo style you're looking for. Simply upload or scan an image of a tattoo or art style you like and let the app do the rest. The app finds the best tattoo artist that works in that style based on the image you uploaded and sorts them based on highest user reviews and ratings. You can view the artist's image gallery, location, contact info and see the reviews left by other people. This reduces the hassle of finding the best artist for the style of tattoo you want.
